About this project

The Struggle is Real.

Project Perfect Memory wants to provide you with something more than just headphones. The perfect sound from 16 Hz to 22000 Hz, elegant design, ecstatically pleasing—all of this we had mastered in our previous generations. This is more. This could save your life.

 

  

 

Release the future!

Imagine you are at school during a lecture and by simply pressing one button, the whole lecture will be recorded. Imagine you are at work. You are meeting a new client and you need to get a picture. Simply press one button and later on you can work right accordingly to their wishes.

Imagine you`re at the concert, on holidays, or meeting an old friend. Imagine—you have a perfect memory.

Our mission.

We set out with a team of programmers to help us make the application handy and easy to use. We created many scenarios about what could went possibly wrong. To secure one's personal space we added a small control light so everybody will know you are recording. But the most difficult was to make the recorded sound almost as good as the one from original recourses. To made the application able to recognise and emphasise voices or sounds over everything else.

And we did it!

Perfect Memory

A diary which you always have with you as a part of your body. Because actually remembering all this much stuff will be too stressful.

 

Why we need your help!

Right now, we can offer you the final prototypes and we need a funding to make Perfect Memory to be part of the world. They majority of costs will be put into making the tool and packaging. If we can hit our goal, we will be able to have Perfect Memory by June of 2017!

 

Risks and challenges

Risks and Challenges are apart of every new product. We currently are utilizing a design firm in Berlin that has been helping company's design and release products for over 4 years. The company also has a team established in China to ensure quality control and clear communication. We have been using this team for the last 6 months and have had no issues in that time. We believe that with the adequate funding the release of Perfect Memory will be seamless and the product will be exactly what consumers expect.
